COBB LOOP LOCAL

Sebastopol · Lake County · Cobb Mountain · Home

~175 mi · ~3.5h · Wine country and mountain roads

Sebastopol → Healdsburg → Hopland → Cobb → Middletown → Calistoga → Sebastopol

A classic Sonoma-Lake County loop starting from Sebastopol. The route heads north through the Alexander Valley wine country before climbing into Lake County via Hopland, winding up to Cobb Mountain — one of the best back-road motorcycle destinations in Northern California. The return sweeps south through Middletown and drops back into Napa County via Calistoga before returning west to Sebastopol through the Sonoma Valley.

⛽ Fuel: Sebastopol · Healdsburg · Hopland · Middletown

⚠️ Hazards: Hwy 175 Cobb Mountain: tight switchbacks, gravel patches at apexes · Hwy 29 Middletown: watch for deer at dusk
